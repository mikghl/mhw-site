About game

"Monster Hunter Wilds" invites players into a vast and immersive world where they embark on epic quests, encounter mythical creatures, and forge their own path as hunters. Set in a breathtaking landscape filled with diverse ecosystems and untold dangers, players will uncover the secrets of the wilds and become part of a rich narrative that unfolds with every challenge faced and every monster conquered.

Features

  • Multiplayer battles
  • Customizable characters
  • Open world exploration
  • Rich storyline
